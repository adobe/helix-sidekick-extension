AEM Sidekick can be enabled or disabled in Settings:

  1. Open Settings app
  2. Tap Safari, then Extensions
  3. Toggle AEM Sidekick

You can turn on AEM Sidekick in Safari Extensions preferences.

AEM Sidekick is currently enabled. You can turn it off in Safari Extensions preferences.

AEM Sidekick is currently disabled. You can turn it on in Safari Extensions preferences.
